Tower Tagger

Solo Developer

A graffiti-themed tower climbing game developed as a solo project.

Tools and Skills Used: Design, Managed Outsourcing, Time Management, Conducted Feedback

Overview

Tower Tagger is a graffiti-themed tower climbing game where players scale urban environments while leaving their mark. As the sole developer, I was responsible for every aspect of the project from design through production.

My Role

As the solo developer on Tower Tagger, I wore every hat — designing the core gameplay, managing outsourced assets, maintaining a development timeline, and conducting feedback sessions to refine the experience.

Responsibilities

  • Design — conceptualized and iterated on the core gameplay mechanics and level design
  • Managed outsourcing — coordinated with external artists and contractors for asset creation
  • Time management — maintained a production schedule to hit key milestones
  • Conducted feedback — organized and ran playtesting sessions to gather player insights
